F.R.I.E.N.D.S

Relax it's not about the American epic dramatic TV show. But I wish things were like that.

I'm probably not in a terrific mood today. Trust me I have my reasons.

You know what I realized, You don't call friends "friends" when you gotta tell them what and why it is about even before calling them.
You call them friends when they pick up your line and starts listening with their full attention and give you their full ears without hesitating and most importantly not raising the Why? question.

Maybe it isn't their fault after all. It might be yours. How right!
for starters, it was you who thought and called them 'friends' in the first place. Bad luck boy. It is you whose choices that made every change to this point. If you drifted away from that particular moment that made you to meet and slowly become friends, you wouldn't have become their friend and probably wouldn't have gotten this hurt.

Time isn't it. Slow as a snail and yet powerful as a knife.
No one asked you for anything and still why are you expecting those from the ones you call friends.

I don't say every one of your friends are like that. Some doesn't bother your call and even loves to talk to you even if they're occupied with their things, and there're some who wouldn't mind calling you back as soon as you leave a missed call.

Deal with it bro. You're the one who failed in choosing the so called right 'friends' and it is not your fault you don't have a friendship like the 6 had in F.R.I.E.N.D.S.

Any Regrets? I would say NEVER coz even though everything seems out of place, We're friends and will always be.

n.b. this was purely about me and me only. I think I criticized myself. Screw it.
