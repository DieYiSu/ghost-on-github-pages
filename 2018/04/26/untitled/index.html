You wanna know why!

  • You should live a life that you may wanna live to please all the people in the world then only you'll understand that there's more to life much more enjoyable than living a life to please everyone.
  • You should do things for the people who can go behind your back that makes you understand people's true double faced fickle mindedness.
  • You should swim ocean for people who won't even cross a bridge for you that way reality hits and leaves you broken like the leaf fell from the tree.
  • You should trust everyone blindly that way you'll know the pain that comes with it.
  • You should expect things from everyone which when they fail you, you get to know that expectations hurt and things n people are temporary.
  • You should be friends with mean people as then you can see how ruthlessly they can bring you to rockbottom.
  • You shouldn't love anyone unconditionally but yourself that way when people you love leave you, you won't be left in vain.
  • You shouldn't feel pain in anyway coz if you do feel it, it'll make you do the dont's and not do the do's as I told here.

Choose happiness over anything. In the end it is what matters and you of all the people in world deserve to love and to be loved to the fullest.
